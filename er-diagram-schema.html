ER Diagram and Schema Design

Introduction

An Entity-Relationship (ER) diagram is a visual representation of the entities in a database and the relationships between them. Schema design involves creating a logical structure of the database using tables, columns, keys, and relationships.

Elements of ER Diagram

Entities

Entities represent objects or things in the database. Each entity is represented by a rectangle.

Attributes

Attributes are properties or characteristics of entities. They are represented by ovals connected to their entity.

Relationships

Relationships represent associations between entities. They are represented by diamonds connected to the related entities.
